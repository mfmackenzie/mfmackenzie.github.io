Progress, not perfection

Hi. I’m Megan! I am on an adventure to a life that is less. 

Less stress, less stuff, less impact. Less. Simple. Easy.

I haven’t always been this way. Like many of you, I was a consumer. I bought unnecessary things, wasted my money, and tried to find happiness through things.

Then one day, I stopped. 

I felt trapped in my house full of things. I felt burdened by how much work is was to have things. I was disgusted by how much trash I was contributing to the planet. And, oh yeah, I was broke.

I decided to stop. And with that decision, I was free. 

Slowly but surely, I am on a mission to downsize my life. To rid myself of the things that don’t make me happy and to place value on people and experiences, not things. 

Through these choices, I am healthier. 
Through these choices, I am producing less trash.
Through these choices, I am spending less money.
Through these choices, I am spending less time dusting knick knacks.
Through these choices, I am becoming more connected with my community.

I’m not an expert, by any means. None of us will ever lead a perfect life. 

But, hot damn, this is the life.
